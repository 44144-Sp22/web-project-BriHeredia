Meet the photographer


Brandon Heredia

Capturing a moment into a compact visual representation of Christ's love is the driving force behind my excitement for photography.

When I take a picture, I get to witness a snapshot of how I believe Christ views us. Capturing a little moment of love and showing the person on the other side of the lens how Christ views them.

My goal with photography is to bring joy through my workspace, and for laughter to be frozen in time
